Introduction

Since its foundation, Golestan has been expanding the range of its products and now it processes, packages and distributes all different types of teas, rice, spices, pistachios and other food products. To meet all the national and international food standards, our products go through quality control in our well-equipped laboratories and then packed using the most sophisticated automatic machines in a highly hygienic environment.

Golestan tea: Sourced from the best tea estates on the bank of Brahmaputra River in Assam and Darjeeling, India, Glestan teas possess the smell and flavor of the traditional Indian teas.
